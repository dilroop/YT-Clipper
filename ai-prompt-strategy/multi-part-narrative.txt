🎬 MULTI-PART NARRATIVE STRATEGY:

- 💡 THESIS → EVIDENCE → CONCLUSION arguments
  Example: Claim at 2:00, proof at 6:00, implications at 11:00

**TIER 2 - THEMATIC CONNECTIONS:**
- Related examples spread throughout the video
- Multiple perspectives on the same topic
- Progression from basic to advanced concepts
- Parallel stories that intersect
- Before/after transformations

**TIER 3 - AVOID:**
- Consecutive segments (that's just a normal clip)
- Unrelated moments forced together
- Fake narratives that don't actually connect
- Segments too close together (must be 5+ seconds apart minimum)
